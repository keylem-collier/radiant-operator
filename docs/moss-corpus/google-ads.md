# Google Ads Strategy Notes

## Search intent mapping

Google Search rewards specificity. Match the query intent exactly — if someone searches "fix dental intake process," do not send them to a generic agency homepage.

High-intent keywords for service businesses:
- "fix lead intake before scaling ads"
- "dental practice marketing agency"
- "controlled growth marketing for dentists"

## Copy principles

- Headline 1: state the outcome ("Fix Intake Before Scaling Ads").
- Headline 2: add credibility or urgency ("For Service Businesses").
- Description: name the tradeoff the buyer actually cares about — capacity, not vanity metrics.

## Performance Max caution

PMax can work but hides where spend goes. For demo clients with messy ops, prefer Search until tracking and intake are solid.

## Landing page alignment

Search copy must match the page above the fold. If the ad says "intake bottleneck," the page cannot say "10x your leads in 30 days."

## Guardrails

- Do not bid on competitor brand terms unless there is a clear differentiation story.
- Do not promise ROAS numbers you cannot defend on a live call.
