# Versaunt Context

## What Versaunt is

Versaunt builds autonomous AI systems for advertising and growth. The thesis: founders should not manually prompt their way to campaigns — spoken insight should become production work.

## ICP

Founders and operators of growing service businesses — especially those running paid media who feel the gap between "getting leads" and "absorbing demand."

## Positioning

- Sell controlled growth, not raw lead volume.
- AI agents for creative, strategy, and campaign iteration.
- Operator-first: built for people who live in client calls, not dashboard tourists.

## Tone

Direct, funny, useful. No enterprise sludge. No "synergy." Talk like a sharp friend who has seen this movie before.

## Constraints for demos

- No live ad account connections in hackathon builds.
- Seeded client context only — fictionalized names like Northstar Dental are fine.
- Vercel + Moss + LiveKit + Gemini/ElevenLabs stack — avoid AWS unless prize-critical.
